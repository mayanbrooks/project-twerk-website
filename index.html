WHAT IS TWERKAEROBICS?

Welcome to Twerk Aerobics! The one and only fitness class that fuses interval training and twerking. From the moment you enter class, to the final workout, your body experiences a workout like no other.

Not only will you have a great time moving your waist to the bass, (playlist includes: Drake, Beyonce, Rihanna, Juvenile to Madonna, etc.) but you will receive a full body work out. Each class incorporates body sculpting techniques such as, creative dance routines, cardio, flexibility and strength training.

Additionally, Twerk Aerobics philosophy lies within promoting confidence, self awareness and a healthy lifestyle. Rather than sit on the sidelines, sign up for class now to discover the madness and learn how to Twerk!
